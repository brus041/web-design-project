There seems to be little to no trends visible in this plot of latitude versus humidity. What can be seen however is that there are a larger amount of points at 100 percent humidity, a moderate amount of points at around average humidity, and little to no data points at very very little humidity. There does seem to be a small cluster of points at latitude 0, as that is significant of the equator. It is well known that areas around the equator, like Florida, California, parts of Africa, and many island nations, are more humid when their annual climates are observed.
